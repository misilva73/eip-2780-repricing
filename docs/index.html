EIP-2780 repricing: TX_BASE & VALUE_GAS

Today every transfer pays a flat 21,000 gas, value or not. EIP-2780 splits that into TX_BASE for a plain transfer and TX_BASE + VALUE_GAS for one that moves value. We fit the per-client, per-case runtime of test_ether_transfers_onchain_receivers with non-negative least squares and convert it to proposed gas at the 100 Mgas/s anchor. Each parameter's worst case is the most expensive driver across all clients and cases.

Preliminary results. Clients are still working on their block-access-list (BAL) optimizations, so these gas values are expected to change as that work lands.
